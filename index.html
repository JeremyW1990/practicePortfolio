About

RESUME

My life events and dev journey.

  • 1999 – 2006

    Programming Enlightenment

    • My story with programming started when I was a kid. I wrote first “Hello World” in QBasic at 3rd Grade. At 7th Grade, I switched to Pascal for 4 years.
    • Started with simple math problem like implementing Gauss formula, ie. 1+2+…100=?
    • Cratched the surface of data algorithm: stack, tree, map, etc.. Tried to solve a problem like finding the shortest path for a mouse to escape a maze.
    • Back then, it was a pure extracurricular hubby I sticked to across my whole childhood. I stopped coding as A-level was hectic.
    • This enlightenment experience matters in my life, as it sets my tone for my life passion.

  • 2010 - 2013

    Electronic Engineering

    • Acquired two engineering degrees in England.
    • Programmed into semiconductors via FPGA.
    • Machines learning: trained it to recognize numbers on license plate images.
    • Algorithmized to detect motion in images, combined them to one ultra-high-resolution image in Fourier domain via Matlab.

  • 2013 - 2015

    Java Developer

    • Surprisingly, I landed my first job as a java developer.
    • Joined HSBC “Graduates Leadership Program”, contributed to the Data Service Layer (DSL) Project - A project aiming at transparentizing investment bank to American financial regulators.
    • Exposed myself with Agile methodology.
    • It was a truly global project with a global team; this experience really broadened my horizon.

  • 2015 - 2017

    Finance

    • I started a new career path in finance.
    • Founded my own private-equity company in Beijing.

  • 2017 - 2019

    Montreal, Canada

    • I was still at a confusing phase of my life: where do I want to live? What should I do?
    • So I moved to Canada, took gap years, resting and thinking about those underlying life questions.
    • Self-Upgrade: workout, meditation, yoga, intermittent fasting.
    • Realized that coding is my real passion for life.

  • Jan, 1st, 2019

    California - final home

    • Once I moved to California, I immediately started to prepare to go back to the dev career path.
    • Consolidated full-stack web development skill set by average 60h/week, 3 months intense training in LearningFuze – one of the best Bootcamp in Irvine.
    • For 3 months, I learned, practiced, dreamed and breathed code.
    • I spent my last 3 weeks there to build a solo final project – a dog walk app, connecting local dog owners and letting them reciprocally walk their dogs.
    • Utilized React, Node.js, MySQL and Google geolocation service in the project.
    • Very first time in my life that I built a full-stack app built from scratch.

  • What's next?

    Keep evolving

    • One philosophy I endorse: live the only life to the maximum.
    • I've been wandering around the world, looking.
    • Experienced a lot and evolved a few times. Now try to settle down in California and dive into the code world.
    • Career takes 1/3 of our lifetime. It weighs significantly in life. I expect to find a company that I can evolve together with.

Dog-CO

A dog-walking app

Walking dog is every dog owners’ daily routine. But owners may not be able to do this every day. A lot of things may get in their way: late working day, road trips, family visit. But your dog is not taking a no for an answer. Going outside or baking at you all night.

It’s an app connecting owners at a local community. If you can walk your own dog, you can walk a few at the same time. By taking turns, dog owners can reduce the tasks from daily to two or three times a week. Set the apps, put information about your dog hand-out time, its walking time preference, etc and wait for the help. Or raise a hand on this app, telling your neighbors that you are able to talk your pet at a certain time and if they need a hand.

  • Date: June 2019
  • Category: Lifestyle
  • Technology: React, NodeJS, Socket.IO, MySQL
